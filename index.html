Frequently asked questions

How do I join my club's team?

Ask your coach or club admin for an invite link or code. Open the link on your phone, sign in or create an account, and you'll be added to the team automatically.

How do I mark my availability for a fixture or training session?

Open the event from your home screen or calendar, then tap "Available" or "Not Available". Coaches can see responses in real time.

I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?

Check that notifications are enabled for Tactiq in your phone's Settings app, and that you're connected to the internet. If the issue continues, contact us at the email above.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account within the app. If you need help, email us and we'll assist you.

How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions sent to your email.
