Tactiq Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: 11 June 2026

Overview

Tactiq ("we", "us", "our") provides a mobile app for sports club management, helping coaches and players organise fixtures, training sessions, and availability. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and profile photo (if provided), including when you sign in with Google.
  • Club and team data: team memberships, roles (coach/player/admin), fixtures, training sessions, and availability responses.
  • Communications: messages, notifications, and reminders sent and received within the app.
  • Payment information: if your club uses paid features, payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your full card details — Stripe handles this securely.
  • Device and usage data: basic diagnostic and crash information collected via Sentry to help us fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Push notification tokens: used to deliver reminders about fixtures, training, and availability requests.

How we use your information

  • To provide core app functionality (scheduling, availability tracking, team coordination).
  • To send notifications and reminders you've opted into.
  • To process payments for paid club features, where applicable.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve the reliability of the app.
  • To respond to support requests.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Other members of your club/team, as needed for coordination (e.g. your name and availability status are visible to your coach and teammates).
  • Service providers who help us operate the app, including Google (sign-in), Stripe (payments), and Sentry (crash/error monitoring).
  • Authorities, where required by law.

Data retention

We retain your account and club data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove or anonymise your personal information, except where we are required to retain it for legal or accounting purposes.

Your rights

You can access, update, or delete your account at any time from within the app (Profile → Settings → Delete Account), or by contacting us directly. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under data protection laws such as the UK GDPR, including the right to request a copy of your data or object to certain processing.

Children's privacy

Tactiq is intended for use by club members, which may include minors participating in youth sports teams, typically managed by a parent, guardian, or coach. We collect only the information necessary for team coordination (e.g. name and availability) and do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at tactiqclub@gmail.com.
